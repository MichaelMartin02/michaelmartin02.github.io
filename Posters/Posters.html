Poster Design Set

Print & Typographic Design


Selected posters created for a variety of purposes. "Division makes life Dull" was created to discuss xenophobia, in participation in the Museum of Typography's 8th annual poster contest. The abstracted nature of the shapes draws connection with the arbitary divisions of nationality, ethnicity, and race that society places upon itself. "Stop and Smell the Smencils" was created as an exercise in re-designing an on campus poster, and "Design Dives" was created to advertise an event in partnership with the KU AIGA Club.
