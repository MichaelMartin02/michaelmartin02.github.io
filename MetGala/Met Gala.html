Met-Gala 2025
Invitation & Album

Typography & Print Design


The theme for the 2025 Met Gala has been revealed to be a celebration of blue-collar work. The Met has distributed themed invitations & albums for the event, invoking the essence of hard labor & struggle. The use of newspaper mimicry & hand-typed, distressed typewriter fonts pays homage to the challenges faced by unions & their members.
