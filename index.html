About Me

Hi, I'm Michael Martin


I’m a communication design graduate from Kutztown University with a passion for coffee, typography, & UI/UX design. I search for projects that allow me to shape an experience, allowing me to better connect with the end user and create more accessible unique, and usable interfaces. My attention to detail ensures no user is left out, making my work accessible and easy to use by anyone. Being driven by deadlines through my academic experience, I have developed essential time management skills that enable me to juggle multiple varied tasks throughout my day. Throughout university, I have become fluent in Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, After effects, and Figma. I have also pushed my skillset to become knowledgeable in working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Processing, and p5.js.
