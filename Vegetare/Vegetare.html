Vegetare
Chocolate

Package Design


Consumer trends indicate a growing interest in healthier alternatives to sweet snacks. To capitalize on this market, Vegetare has developed a line of nutritious chocolate products infused with vegetables and vitamins. The brand identity employs organic typography, colored paper, & vegetable imagery to effectively highlight these health benefits.
