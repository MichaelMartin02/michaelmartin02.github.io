Branding & Identity


As the world continues to globalize & the younger generation shows a growing interest in exploring diverse cultures, there is an increasing demand for restaurants that embrace & promote foreign food and drinking traditions. Masida's mission is to seamlessly blend two distinct Korean subcultures—the modern coffee culture & the traditional alcoholic beverage drinking culture—& introduce them to a Western audience.
