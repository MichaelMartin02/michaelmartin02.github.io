Sonora Cacti Leather

Branding & Identity


As the world transitions away from traditional fabrics, which worsen climate change & can give way to animal cruelty, a void in the market needs to be filled to with an alternative material. Sonora Cactus Leathers seeks to meet the leather market where its at, creating a plant-based cowhide competitor made from the pads of the prickly pear cactus. As a B2B company, Sonora supplies fashion brands, book publishers, & other companies which rely on leather with a sustainably grown & manufactured material.
