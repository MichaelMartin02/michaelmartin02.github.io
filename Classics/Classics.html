Re-designed Classics

Publication, UI/UX Design & Prototyping


My capstone project focused on revamping classic short stories commonly taught in English classrooms to enhance accessibility for younger audiences. Traditional distributions of these stories in their original formats by teachers & libraries often present challenges for students. Therefore, I treated the two iconic works, "The Yellow Wallpaper" & "Sonny's Blues," as if they were being designed today, completely reimagining their publication. These redesigned versions feature modern layouts & formats, aiming to make them more engaging & approachable for secondary students & parents. The goal was to provide these redesigned prints to educators & libraries at a minimal cost. A process paper was written for this project & can be found here.
